Privacy Policy

NE Claude Plugins — Last updated: April 6, 2026

Overview

NE Claude Plugins is a collection of open-source plugins for Claude Code. All plugins run locally on your machine. Nextera Labs does not operate any servers, collect any telemetry, or receive any data from plugin users.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. No analytics, no telemetry, no crash reporting.

Third-Party Services

Some plugins interact with third-party services at your direction:

  • discord-notify — sends messages to Discord channels you configure. Subject to Discord's Privacy Policy.
  • obsidian-vault — reads and writes files in your local Obsidian vault. No data leaves your machine.
  • codesmith, code-audit, skillforge, secret-guard, drawio — operate entirely within your local Claude Code session. No external network calls.

Claude / Anthropic

These plugins run inside Claude Code. Your interactions with Claude are subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy.

Open Source

All plugin code is publicly available at github.com/nexteralabs/ne-claude-plugins. You can audit exactly what each plugin does.
